    Unit 1, Outcome 2

    2.3 Assess the strengths and weaknesses of your final project – are you happy with your website? Why? Why not? You should review it on the website itself in the interests of openness. What would you change?

    Overall, I’m not at all satisfied with the outcome of this website - I feel that as it stands, it wouldn’t achieve my primary objective of showcasing the level of skill required from a prospective Technical Consultant by the clients or companies I would like to work with. It only takes a quick look at awwwards.com to see that the bar is raised fairly high!

    Due to current skill limitations and time restraints, I’ve had to compromise on a few design features and testing as it would have taken too long to research solutions online, and skip the UX/UI planning process - which I believe is a crucial step in my personal workflow.

    Given the opportunity, there are several improvements I could make around site features, user experience and interface design (UX/UI) especially with regard to accessibility, responsiveness and testing.
