    Unit 1, Outcome 3

    3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency.

    As before, I would have adopted a more focussed approach to executing tasks and employed better strategies to manage my ADHD in order to improve time management.

    Unfortunately further extenuating circumstances around a death had affected my ability to concentrate fully on completing outstanding tasks. Although I agree that this should not have affected the work required to meet the qualification criteria, it unfortunately did nonetheless. I’m not sure how I would improve on this in future to mitigate its impact, other than to hope that it doesn’t happen again.

    Being strict on screen breaks and trying to achieve tasks within the working day has been helpful in avoiding burnout, and thereby maintaining productivity to some degree. However I did notice that I began falling behind other classmates who worked through their breaks and put in additional time after class - perhaps this is unavoidable in meeting set goals which require a certain commitment of time and energy.

    In terms of personal productivity, I also found it useful to be in a comfortable working environment at home, where I haven’t had to queue for lunch/shared facilities or waste time and energy on a commute. I did initially struggle a little with my second screen plus Bluetooth wireless keyboard setup since I kept losing windows between the two screens, and the Bluetooth keyboard kept having to be activated before typing - which led to a few minor coding errors. A better home office set up with a more ergonomic chair and desk would also have helped to improve productivity and efficiency.
